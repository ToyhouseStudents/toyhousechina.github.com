Our Workflow

The following graph tells how we collaborate with each other. If you can not understand it, please start leaning git and github. After learning, if you still can not understand, just find a window or a wall. You know it.

Organizations must DO

  • Write Wiki for any features you add to repos.
  • Manage your teams.
  • Pull Request to ToyhouseChina when your repos are stable.

Right way for Organizations

  • Always fork repos from Toyhousechina.
  • Assign repos to proper teams in your organization (actually, there only two teams: owners and TeamMembers).
  • Give the right rights for each team.
  • Take the System Architecture organization as an example.

Right way for Individuals

  • You can fork from ToyhouseChina individually BUT we won't merge for you. If you fork repos of your own organizations, please go to your team leader and ask him or her to assign repos to you. Actually, there is no need for individuals to fork repos because you should be assigned some repos.
  • You should use the assigned repo by your organization and make sure that you have pull & push rights, otherwise, just go to your team leader for the rights.
